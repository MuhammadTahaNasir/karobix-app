# 📱 Karobix - Apka Digital Munshi

**Karobix** is a powerful, AI-driven business accounting and management app designed specifically for small businesses and shops in Pakistan. Move away from traditional paper registers and manage your sales, stock, and credits with the power of your voice.

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-Landing_Page-emerald?style=for-the-badge)](https://muhammadtahanasir.github.io/karobix-app/)
[![Release](https://img.shields.io/badge/Release-v1.0.0-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/MuhammadTahaNasir/karobix-app/releases)

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **🎙️ Munshi AI 2.0**: The heart of the app. Speak to the app in Urdu, English, or Roman Urdu to record sales and stock. No typing needed!
- **📒 Digital Khata & Udhar**: Manage customer credits and send professional WhatsApp reminders to get paid 2x faster.
- **📄 PDF Invoicing**: Generate professional receipts and invoices instantly and share them with your customers.
- **📊 Smart Analytics**: View beautiful charts of your daily and monthly profits. Know exactly how your business is performing.
- **🛡️ Biometric Security**: Protect your sensitive business data with Fingerprint and Face ID locks.
- **☁️ Cloud Sync**: Work offline and have your data automatically backed up to the cloud.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Flutter (Dart)
- **Backend**: Firebase (Firestore & Auth)
- **AI Engine**: Groq AI (Llama 3)
- **State Management**: Provider
- **Local Storage**: SQLite / Hive
